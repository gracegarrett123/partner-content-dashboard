Move: lead health-system and pharma pitches with dwell, and keep building the sponsored healthcare-audio slate off the podcast. Adobe

            Automotive News holds #2 on reach — 5,363 visits (12.1% of the portfolio), carried by the connected-dealer-tech and car-buying reads; dwell stays thin (1:21 avg). Move: keep selling Auto News on reach and dealer-tech/mobility targeting, not engagement. Adobe

            Portfolio is down ~3.5% WoW, and Ad Age is most of it — 44,385 visits vs 45,995 last report; Ad Age alone fell 1,240 visits (-8.8%) as last week's World Cup/sports-fandom surge didn't repeat, plus Grand Rapids (-9.3%) and Hart Energy (-23.5%, off a small base). Move: check Ad Age's sponsored content cadence before the next client report — this reads as a calendar gap, not softening demand (Rubber News +14.2% and Auto News +1.0% both held up). Adobe

            Sponsored still out-dwells editorial — per the last Tableau pull (extract as of Jul 13; today's did not refresh), sponsored content is ~1.3% of pageviews yet posts a slightly higher median dwell than editorial. Move: keep selling dwell and audience quality over reach; re-confirm once today's extract is back online. Tableau

            Concentration is still the structural risk — MH + Ad Age = 61.4% of traffic; add Automotive News and the top 3 = 73.1%. The six city brands, Hart and the trade titles split the rest. Move: keep building the mid-tier reach bundle (Auto News + P&I + Chicago) to de-risk the two-brand dependence. Adobe

            ⚠️ Watch Areas

            Volume without engagement — verify before a sponsor deck. Cleveland's Centric Consulting AI-agents read drew 592 visits at a 0:18 dwell, still dragging the brand to 1:16. A bounce pattern, not a read. Avg time is a proxy for engagement, not literal read time. Move: confirm placement/tagging before it's reported as performance. Adobe

            The trade brands stay thin and low-dwell — and some of their reads are directional only. Tire Business (281 visits / 0:47), Rubber News (636 / 1:15) and Plastics News (1,767 / 1:22) are real inventory but not heavy hitters; several of their #2–5 stories sit on single-digit visit counts. Move: package them as niche/vertical buys, treat the sub-50-visit reads as directional, and don't slot them next to Ad Age/MH reach. Adobe

            Measurement debt: we still can't see where ~half the audience comes from. Move: fix UTM/referrer tagging, re-verify once the extract is back, and make no channel-attribution claims in client decks until then. Tableau

            High dwell at scale — amplify these. Ad Age's YouTube Creators read (5:15 / 610), MH's UPMC Payvider profile (3:15 / 1,218) and the MH Cancer Immunotherapy podcast (2:25 / 3,427) all clear strong dwell at real volume. Move: feature on homepage + newsletter as sales proof. Adobe

            📖 Metrics & caveats Adobe Tableau

            Visits / Page Views / Unique Visitors — from Adobe (site) and Tableau (sponsored, cumulative). Unique = distinct users in the window; page views exclude same-session repeats.

            Avg time on page — depth-of-engagement proxy. It is not listen/read time and (for podcasts) not download-based; individual listen data isn't available across platforms.

            Benchmark / pacing — actual vs the content-type benchmark (Campaign Performance master, current quarter) prorated to the window; where no benchmark exists we compare to the portfolio 2:09 average or prior week, and we name the comparator. ±15% = on pace.

            Not yet measured — audience firmographics (company size, industry, named accounts), intent (Bombora), traffic-source mix, and podcast downloads (Libsyn). These require new sources and are the highest-value add for client-grade insights.
