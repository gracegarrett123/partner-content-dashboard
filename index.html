        💡 Key Insights

        Modern Healthcare beats the engagement bar at scale — 12,797 visits (39% of portfolio) at a 2:45 avg, ~28% above the 2:09 portfolio average; its Cancer Immunotherapy podcast is #1 overall (3,846). Move: lead health-system/pharma pitches with dwell (not reach) and commission a sponsored healthcare-audio slate. Adobe

        Ad Age is the dwell-at-scale proof point — TV's Underrated Role held 3:08 over 1,197 visits (~47% above the 2:09 average) and the CES AI/TV read hit 4:19. Move: package a "video influence" buy for platforms/ad-tech and use these as the reach-plus-depth case study. Adobe

        P&I vs its content benchmark: only 1 of 5 on pace — against Partner Content (~460 PV expected/7d), the Long-Only AI-Era read paces +13%; Systematic Macro and Fixed Income sit 15–17% under. Move: repromote the under-pacing reads before flighting new P&I partner content. Adobe

        Quality up, volume flat — portfolio +0.7% WoW (32,476), but five reads cleared a 3:00 dwell at 500+ visits. Move: lead sponsor reporting with dwell + benchmark pacing, not impressions — that is what the client decks respond to. Adobe

        Concentration is both risk and opportunity — MH + Ad Age ≈ 74% of traffic; the six city brands + Hart ≈ 17%. Move: build a city-brand vertical bundle (finance/CRE/workforce) and cross-sell the MH/Ad Age audience into it. Adobe Smartsheet

        ⚠️ Watch Areas

        Extreme dwell on tiny volume — verify before it reaches a client deck. Several reads show 6–13 min "dwell" on <65 visits (e.g., Hart audiovisual white paper 6:26 / 62). Caveat: avg time on page ≠ read/listen time — treat <50-visit reads as directional. Move: verify before sponsor reporting. Adobe

        High dwell at scale — amplify — MH Expanding Lung Transplant (4:00 / 834), Ad Age TV's Underrated Role (3:08 / 1,197) and MH Leading Cancer Institutions (3:55 / 1,185) clear 3:00 at real volume. Move: feature on homepage + newsletter as sales proof. Adobe

        Hub-page leak wastes sponsor spend — Grand Rapids routes ~18% of visits to the generic "crain's content studio" landing page. Move: deep-link the featured reads so paid promotion lands on sponsored content. Adobe

        Attribution gaps block upsell — generic hub names ("crain's content studio," "publishing partners") still surface in top reads (Grand Rapids, Cleveland). Move: clean tagging to unlock accurate attribution and upsell. Adobe

        Biggest gap: no audience layer — we can't yet show who engaged (company size, industry, named accounts, intent) or podcast downloads — the metrics clients value most (see the Warner Bros deck). Move: wire in Libsyn + Bombora + Adobe firmographic/traffic-source reports. gap

        Ideas & next moves — organized by lane. Each card: evidence → opportunity → the move, spanning new content, partnerships, products, marketing, and audience.

        📖 Metrics & caveats Adobe Tableau

        Visits / Page Views / Unique Visitors — from Adobe (site) and Tableau (sponsored, cumulative). Unique = distinct users in the window; page views exclude same-session repeats.

        Avg time on page — depth-of-engagement proxy. It is not listen/read time and (for podcasts) not download-based; individual listen data isn't available across platforms.

        Benchmark / pacing — actual vs the content-type benchmark (Campaign Performance master, current quarter) prorated to the window; where no benchmark exists we compare to the portfolio 2:09 average or prior week, and we name the comparator. ±15% = on pace.

        Not yet measured — audience firmographics (company size, industry, named accounts), intent (Bombora), traffic-source mix, and podcast downloads (Libsyn). These require new sources and are the highest-value add for client-grade insights.
